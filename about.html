About Us

At Dhukuti, we bring you the finest handmade and natural products from across Nepal — each one rooted in tradition and crafted with care.

We partner directly with skilled artisans, small producers, and local communities from the mountains to the plains. Every item we offer reflects the rich cultural heritage and craftsmanship of Nepal, passed down through generations.

Our mission is simple:
To celebrate tradition, empower communities, and build a sustainable future.

By choosing our products, you’re not just supporting local makers — you’re helping preserve age-old skills, create fair income opportunities, and promote eco-friendly living. Most of what we offer is made using sustainable materials, keeping nature and well-being at the heart of every creation.
